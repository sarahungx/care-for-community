San Francisco Hotline Numbers


Please consult the following phone numbers for assistance.


San Francisco Homeless Outreach Team

➳

Call 415-355-7401

Call this number for street outreach or medical help.

311 San Francisco

➳

Call 311 or 415-701-2311 (TTY 415-701-2323)

Call this number to reserve a bed at a shelter for 90 days.

211 San Francisco

➳

Call 211 or 1-800-273-6222

Call this number for food and shelter services.

Homeless Advocacy Project

➳

Call 1-800-405-4HAP (4427)

Call this number to recieve legal services.
